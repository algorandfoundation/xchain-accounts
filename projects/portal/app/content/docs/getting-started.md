# Getting Started

Get up and running with Algorand x EVM in a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

- **EVM wallet** like Metamask, Coinbase Wallet, Rainbow, etc.
- Stablecoins (USDC, USDT, or USDe) on any supported EVM chain **or** a small amount of ALGO - see [Fund Your Account](/docs/fund-account) for all options

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Go to App

Navigate to an Algorand x EVM compatible dApp, or use this site's [Manage Account](/app) interface.

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

Click **Connect Wallet** and select your preferred EVM wallet. You'll see your EVM address and your derived Algorand address.

### 3. Fund Your Account

Your derived Algorand address starts with zero balance. The easiest way to fund it is to **bridge stablecoins from an EVM chain**:

- Open the **Bridge** panel, select your source chain and amount, and approve in your wallet

Alternatively, you can send ALGO directly:

- Use the **Receive** panel to copy your Algorand address or scan the QR code
- Send ALGO from an exchange, the TestNet faucet, or another Algorand wallet

See [Fund Your Account](/docs/fund-account) for full details on all funding options.

### 4. Start Transacting

Once funded, you can:

- **Send** ALGO or ASAs to any Algorand address
- **Receive** by sharing your derived address
- **Bridge** assets to and from EVM chains
- **Manage** your opted-in assets

Each transaction will prompt your wallet to sign an EIP-712 typed data message scoped to the Algorand x EVM system.
